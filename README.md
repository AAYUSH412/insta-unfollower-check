## 📱 Overview

**Instagram Unfollower Checker** is a free, privacy-focused web tool that helps you analyze your Instagram connections. In just a few clicks, you can:

- 👁️ **Find who doesn't follow you back**
- 🔄 **Discover mutual followers**
- 📊 **Analyze your follower-to-following ratio**
- 🔍 **Search through your Instagram relationships**

All processing happens locally in your browser - **your data never leaves your device!**

## 🚀 How To Use

### Step 1: Download Your Instagram Data

<details>
<summary>Click to expand detailed instructions</summary>

1. Open Instagram app and go to your profile
2. Tap ≡ (menu) → Settings and privacy → Account Center
3. Select "Your information and permissions"
4. Choose "Download your information"
5. Select "Download or transfer information"
6. Choose your Instagram account
7. Select "Some of your information" 
8. Check "Followers and following" data
9. Choose JSON format
10. Select "Download to device"
11. Create file and wait for Instagram to process (typically takes 1-48 hours)
12. Download the zip file when ready

</details>

### Step 2: Upload Your Files

1. Extract the downloaded zip file from Instagram
2. Find and upload `followers_1.json` to our tool
3. Find and upload `following.json` to our tool
4. Click "Compare Files" to see your results

### Step 3: Explore Your Data

- View users who don't follow you back
- Check your mutual followers
- Search for specific users
- Analyze your follower ratio

## 💻 Installation and Local Development

### Prerequisites

- Node.js (v16+)
- npm or yarn

### Setup

1. **Clone the repository**
   ```bash
   git clone https://github.com/yourusername/insta_unfollower_list.git
   cd insta_unfollower_list/frontend
   ```

2. **Install dependencies**
   ```bash
   npm install
   # or with yarn
   yarn install
   ```

3. **Start development server**
   ```bash
   npm run dev
   # or with yarn
   yarn dev
   ```

4. **Open browser at [http://localhost:5173](http://localhost:5173)**

## 🔍 FAQs

<details>
<summary><strong>Is this tool safe to use?</strong></summary>
Yes! All data processing happens locally in your browser. Your Instagram data never leaves your device, and we don't have servers that store or process your information.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Do I need to log in with my Instagram account?</strong></summary>
No. You only need to download your Instagram data and upload the specific JSON files. We never ask for or need your Instagram credentials.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Is this affiliated with Instagram/Meta?</strong></summary>
No. This is an independent tool and is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or connected to Instagram or Meta in any way.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>What if I can't find the JSON files?</strong></summary>
After downloading and extracting your Instagram data, look for a folder called "followers_and_following". Inside, you should find "followers_1.json" and "following.json" files.
</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Does this tool store my data?</strong></summary>
No. All processing happens in your browser. No data is stored on servers or persisted after you close the browser tab.
</details>
